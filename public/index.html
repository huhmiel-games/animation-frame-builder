    Animation Frame Builder

    @huhmiel-games 2026

    Animation Frame Builder is a simple and easy to use graphics tool that help you creates frames from atlas without json.

    Usage:
    Open an image on the left panel.
    Select an area with the mouse on the left image (default snap grid to 8px), the selection is copied to a new frame on the right panel.
    Or enable the grid and adjust its size and offset, just clic a cell and the selection is copied to a new frame on the right panel.

    Enjoy ...

    Commands:
    Select
    Reset selection
    Scroll Y
    Shift + Scroll X
    Ctrl + Zoom

    Enjoy...
