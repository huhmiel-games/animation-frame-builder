    Spritesheet-Preparer

    @huhmiel-games 2023

    Spritesheet-Preparer is a simple and easy to use graphics tool that help you creates spritesheet from atlas without json. images.

    Usage:
    Open an image on the left panel.
    Open a tileset on the right panel.
    Select an area with the mouse on the left image (offset the image if needed), copy is automatic, just click on your tileset to paste it.
    Repeat ...

    Commands:
    Select or Paste
    Reset selection
    Scroll Y
    Shift + Scroll X
    Ctrl + Zoom
    Ctrl + ASelect all
    Ctrl + ZUndo
    Ctrl + YRedo
    Ctrl + SSave
    XFlip selection horizontally
    YFlip selection vertically

    Enjoy...
